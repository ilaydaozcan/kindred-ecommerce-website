About Maya

I'm a mixed media artist based in Portland, Oregon. My work explores the intersection of natural light, texture, and emotion — heavily influenced by the Pacific Northwest landscape and years of travel through Southeast Asia and Southern Europe.

Before going full-time on Kindred, I worked as a graphic designer for 6 years. Making the leap to independent art felt terrifying, but this community made it possible. Today, I spend my days painting, teaching workshops, and sharing my process with the people who've supported this journey.

What you'll get here: behind-the-scenes looks at my studio practice, process breakdowns, early access to new collections, and a community of people who genuinely love art. I post 3-4 times per week and do a live studio session every Friday for Inner Circle members and above.
