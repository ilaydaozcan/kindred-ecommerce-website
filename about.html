Our mission

Make a living doing what you love

We believe the internet has created an extraordinary opportunity — anyone with something worth sharing can find the people who care. But the tools to do that sustainably have lagged far behind the ambition of creators.

Kindred exists to close that gap. We build infrastructure for human connection: the memberships, the community spaces, the payment rails, and the support that lets creators focus on their work instead of the business of their work.

Our north star is simple — when creators thrive, culture thrives.

What we believe

Four things we'll never compromise on

01

Creators come first

Every product decision starts with one question: does this help creators do more of what they love? If the answer is no, we don't ship it.

02

Radical transparency

Our fee is 5%. It's on our homepage. No tiers, no fine print, no percentage that changes once you're hooked. What you see is what you get.

03

Real relationships

Followers are numbers. Supporters are people. We build tools for depth, not scale — because 200 true fans beat 20,000 passive ones.

04

Long-term thinking

We're not optimizing for viral moments. We're building durable income streams and communities that last. Slow, steady, and sustainable wins.

Our story

How we got here

2021

The idea sparks

Co-founders Nadia Park and Felix Osei — both former creators — launch Kindred out of a shared frustration: platforms were keeping too much and giving too little. They raise a $3.2M seed round and start building.

2022

First 1,000 creators

After a quiet beta, Kindred hits 1,000 active creators — faster than any comparable platform in its first year. Word spreads through creative communities organically. No ads, just momentum.

2023

Community tools launch

We ship posts, polls, direct messaging, and exclusive content spaces — a full community home built inside Kindred. $18M Series A closes. The team grows to 22 people across 6 countries.

2024

$100M milestone

Kindred creators collectively earn their 100 millionth dollar. We mark the moment by lowering our platform fee from 8% to 5% — and promise it will never go back up.

Today

50K+ creators and growing

We're in 40+ countries, with 50,000 creators and 2 million supporters. We're still the same team who got mad about the same problem — just with more tools, more people, and more conviction than ever.
