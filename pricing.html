FAQ

Got questions?

What happens after my free trial?

Your 14-day free trial gives you full access to all Pro or Business features. After it ends, you'll be billed at your chosen plan rate. You can downgrade to Starter anytime — no questions asked.

What's the platform fee?

Each plan has a small transaction fee on supporter payments (8% Starter, 5% Pro, 3% Business). This covers payment processing, fraud protection, and platform maintenance. There are no hidden fees.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel whenever you want. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be credited for the remainder. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

How do payouts work?

Starter creators get monthly payouts. Pro unlocks weekly payouts, and Business gets instant payouts. We support direct deposit, PayPal, and Wise for international creators in 40+ countries.

Do supporters pay extra?

Nope. Supporters pay exactly the tier price you set. All platform and transaction fees come from the creator's side, so the experience is seamless for your community.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts, ever. All plans are month-to-month (or annual if you choose the discount). Cancel anytime and your page stays active through the end of your billing period.
